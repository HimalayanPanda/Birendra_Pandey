Biography & Leadership

About

Birendra Raj Pandey

Senior Vice President, Confederation of Nepalese Industries (CNI) · Managing Director, Nepal Adarsha Nirman Company

Mr. Pandey is recognized for delivering complex infrastructure, advancing sustainable construction, and shaping public–private collaboration in Nepal. Since April 2011, he has led Nepal Adarsha Nirman Company, introducing technology‑driven project management and ESG‑aligned practices to improve delivery quality and reliability across 500+ projects nationwide (roads and bridges, water supply, buildings, seismic retrofitting, and cultural heritage restoration).

Leadership & Industry

  • Senior Vice President, CNI; Coordinator, Infrastructure Committee: Organizes Nepal Infrastructure Summits (2014, 2017, 2019, 2022, 2024) and leads policy dialogue on energy, transport, PPPs, and ESG.
  • FCAN: Active since 1993; Advisor since Dec 2018.
  • IFAWPCA: Leadership roles since 2005; former Secretary‑General.
  • YCNC: Co‑founder (2013); President (Jan 2016 – Sep 2018).

International Experience

  • Reed Constructions Australia (Commercial Manager): Managed contractual and financial controls for major works.
  • Georgiou Group, Australia (Project & Engineering Manager): Led planning, execution, and handover of infrastructure projects.

Education & Honors

  • M.Eng., Construction Engineering & Project Management, AIT (1999)
  • MBA, Tribhuvan University (1997); B.E. Civil (1993)
  • AIT Distinguished Alumni Award (2020); multiple academic scholarships

Vision

Accelerate Nepal’s infrastructure‑led growth through ESG‑aligned procurement, sound project governance, and TVET reforms that align skills with industry and export markets.
