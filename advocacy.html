Policy & Advocacy

Industry Advocacy

As Senior Vice President of CNI and Coordinator of the Infrastructure Committee, Mr. Pandey has convened successive Nepal Infrastructure Summits (2014, 2017, 2019, 2022, 2024) to align government, private sector, and development partners on bankable projects, ESG standards, and investor‑ready PPP frameworks. He leads high‑level engagements on energy policy reform, private participation in power, and infrastructure‑led industrial growth.

  • Created Infrastructure Cell and CNI Research Cell focused on infrastructure development and sustainability
  • Led studies and published reports on Nepal Infrastructure 2030 (energy, transport, water & sanitation, urban development)
  • Facilitated dialogues with policy institutions and development partners to strengthen Nepal’s investment climate

Birendra Raj Pandey

President, CNI | Industry Leader & Strategic Advisor
