About

Birendra Raj Pandey

President, Confederation of Nepalese Industries (CNI) · Executive Director, Nepal Adarsha Nirman Company

With three decades of experience in infrastructure and strategic industry leadership, Mr. Birendra Raj Pandey is a driving force behind Nepal's private‑sector growth and industrial competitiveness.

Current Leadership

  • President, CNI (2025– ): Elected unanimously at CNI's 22nd Annual General Meeting, after serving as Senior Vice President since 2018. He now leads Nepal's apex industrial body, advocating reforms to grow the national economy to USD 100 billion and achieve double‑digit growth within a decade.
  • Executive Director, Nepal Adarsha Nirman Company Pvt. Ltd. (NANC): A civil‑engineering and infrastructure veteran with expertise in roads, water resources, green materials, structural retrofits, and urban development.
  • Strategic Sustainability Leader: Led major MoU with AIT (January 2025) to develop ESG frameworks and entrepreneurship-focused training programs.
  • Builder of Institutional Governance: Initiated formation of 19 sector-specific CNI committees and forums, including Infrastructure, Education and Skills, Green Business, Diaspora Engagement, and Startups.

Education & Credentials

ME in Construction Engineering & Project Management from the Asian Institute of Technology, Thailand (1999), and MBA from Tribhuvan University (1997). Trained by global institutions including Shimizu Corporation (Japan), IIT Delhi, and NUST (Norway). He holds leadership roles in professional bodies like the Nepal Engineering Council, FCAN, Rotary International, and Samarpan Academy.

Vision

To foster sustainable industrial growth in Nepal through innovation, ESG frameworks, and strategic collaboration across government, academia, and industry.
